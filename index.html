Video & Audio Editing

I’ve been editing videos for over 4 years and have built solid experience using tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, Audition, CapCut, and OBS Studio. I use Premiere Pro for all my video edits and Audition to handle audio post-production with clarity and precision. CapCut is especially useful for speech-to-text conversion and creating fast, engaging short-form content like TikToks and YouTube Shorts. I'm experienced with everything from long-form videos and podcasts to short, punchy clips for social media. I also use OBS for screen recording and streaming setups. In addition to editing, I design custom thumbnails for the content I create, which you’ll see in the next section.
